WHO WE ARE?

Poclane Construction Equipment India Pvt. Ltd. brings you advanced construction machinery designed for high performance, reliability, and efficiency. With over three decades of industry experience and partnerships with leading technology providers, Poclane India serves as a trusted source for top-tier excavators and lifting equipment, driving infrastructure development across India and globally. As a subsidiary of a global leader in construction equipment, automotive technology, engine power, and hydraulics, Poclane India operates with the support of a $100 billion USD turnover parent company. With a network of over 2,500 technical and R&D experts, Poclane India is committed to manufacturing excellence and innovation. Our state-of-the-art production facility spans 170,000 square meters, achieving an annual capacity of over 20,000 units, positioning Poclane as a global leader in construction machinery.
