ABOUT US

At Poclane Construction Equipment India Pvt. Ltd., we are committed to delivering innovative and reliable construction machinery solutions that meet the evolving demands of the infrastructure and heavy equipment industry. Backed by decades of expertise and a commitment to excellence, we offer a diverse range of high-performance machinery designed to support various sectors, including construction, mining, and material handling. Our state-of-the-art equipment is crafted using cutting-edge technology, ensuring exceptional reliability, fuel efficiency, and enhanced productivity. Built with top-tier components from global leaders like Cummins, Kawasaki, and KYB, Poclane products are engineered to withstand the most challenging environments while delivering precision and durability. Customer Satisfaction and Operational Efficiency We prioritize customer satisfaction and operational excellence by providing not only world-class products but also comprehensive after-sales support. Our customer-centric approach includes reliable after-sales service, operator and technical training programs, and a robust spare parts supply network. Poclane is dedicated to empowering users with the skills and resources needed to maximize their equipment’s potential. Your Trusted Partner in Construction Machinery Poclane is your trusted partner, combining performance, quality, and customer-focused solutions to create a strong foundation for your success. Whether in urban development, mining operations, or road construction, our advanced machinery helps you achieve your project goals with efficiency, precision, and confidence. Choose Poclane for industry-leading construction machinery that drives productivity and fuels progress, one project at a time.
